## Page Outlines

**Purpose:** First impression. Hook in 5 seconds. Drive to portfolio.

**Sections:**

1. **Hero**
   - Headline: Name + Title
   - Subhead: One-line value prop with key achievement
   - CTA: "View My Work" or "See Portfolio"
   - Optional: Professional photo

2. **Quick Stats** (optional)
   - 6 years Shopify experience
   - $2M+/year at Quality Sewing
   - 5K → 22K organic traffic growth
   - Currently E-commerce Manager at Quality Sewing

3. **Featured Work** (3 projects max)
   - Thumbnail + title + one-line result
   - Links to full case studies

4. **CTA Section**
   - "Looking for your next hire?" or similar

**Word Count:** ~150-200 words total (brief)

### Portfolio Case Study Template

**Purpose:** Deep dive into a single project. Prove skills with specifics.

**Sections:**

1. **Header**
   - Project name
   - One-line description
   - Hero image/screenshot

2. **Overview**
   - What is this project?
   - Your role
   - Timeline

3. **The Challenge**
   - What problem were you solving?
   - What were the goals?

4. **The Solution**
   - What did you do?
   - What tools/strategies did you use?
   - Key decisions made

5. **Results**
   - Metrics (revenue, traffic, growth)
   - Screenshots of dashboards/analytics
   - Before/after if applicable

6. **Key Takeaways**
   - What you learned
   - What you'd do differently

7. **CTA**
   - View next project
   - Contact me

**Word Count:** ~500-800 words per case study

## Technical Notes

### URLs
- Keep URLs short and clean
- Use lowercase, hyphens
- Example: /portfolio/luxury-wall-art

### Images
- Compress all images
- Use descriptive alt text
- Consistent aspect ratios for thumbnails

### Mobile
- Design mobile-first
- Test all pages on phone
- Ensure forms work on touch

---

## Questions to Answer

Before building, clarify:

1. **Platform:** Stay on Shopify or switch to something simpler (Webflow, Squarespace, custom)?
2. **Primary goal:** Job search or freelance clients?
3. **Available metrics:** What numbers can you actually share publicly?
4. **Timeline:** When do you need this live?
5. **Budget:** Any budget for premium themes/tools?
